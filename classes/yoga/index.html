Yoga Classes

How long have you been hearing about the benefits of yoga? We all know yoga is great for promoting relaxation and alleviating stress, as well as improving posture, alignment, and breathing. In addition to those advantages, yoga also improves balance, core strength, focus and mental acuity, and can help support weight loss efforts and athletic goals.

Now is the perfect time to integrate yoga into your fitness regimen. At Lateral Fitness, we offer a variety of yoga styles, all led by our expert team of instructors. Whether you are an experienced yogi looking for a new challenge, an endurance athlete seeking a practice to help recover and restore from your latest endeavor, or a yoga newbie wishing to explore the practice in an intimidation-free environment, we have a class for you! Talk to any member of our friendly staff about which yoga style is best suited to your needs, and enjoy taking your practice to the next level in an intimate, boutique space with premium mats and props. Namaste!

  • Barefoot Bootcamp All Levels
    This class infuses the heart-pumping qualities of classical calisthenics, body-weight exercises, plyometrics and traditional yoga inspired sun salutations to strengthen and tone your entire body. If you want to achieve the fitness benefits of a bootcamp without all the screaming and yelling, enlist in Barefoot Bootcamp!
  • Core Inferno All Levels
    You will surely feel the burn in this heated core class. Using core strengthening techniques from Floor Pilates, yoga and Barre classes, you will heat up from the inside out. Combinations and exercises target deep muscles, toning and strengthening the abdomen. The room will also be heated to around 85 degrees.
  • Vinyasa Yoga All Levels
    This flow class is all about connecting breath (pranayama) to movement (asana). Expect a mix of vigorous standing postures and balances with heart opening back bends and hip opening postures, ending with a deep relaxation. Through the practice you will focus your mind, challenge your body, and ease stress. Each class will be structured differently by our expert instructors, so you’ll never get bored. Basic yoga experience suggested. (We also have Beginner Vinyasa and Power Vinyasa - for the advanced as well)
  • Yin Athletica All Levels
    Yin Athletica is a deep restorative class with long passive stretching sequences which includes some prone and kneeling postures. Addressing the opening and release of the major joints: deep spinal twists, profound shoulder and hip openers, to access the deeper layer of tissue (fascia) done with long 3 - 5 minute holds. Following a yin class, your body can feel deeply relaxed and often the effects of the release can last long after, releasing deep seated tension and emotions. Come enjoy and discover as you unfold! This class is for all levels for the 'athlete' in all of us! (We also offer a Yin Yoga class for the non-athlete)
  • Yoga Sculpt All Levels
    This class is a combination of yoga inspired movements and free weights for increased muscle endurance, strength, stamina, and flexibility. Our Yoga Sculpt is a total body workout designed to tone or ‘sculpt’ every major muscle group. Free weights serve as your own personal adjuster as you move through sun salutations and other yoga postures, enabling extra length and depth in each pose. Strength training is great way to build lean muscle, which in return, will have greater long term impact on your metabolism, even while resting.

Class Policy

Please show up 15 minutes before the start of the class for equipment set up. Since Lateral Fitness wants to keep each class moving smoothly you are not allowed to show up after the start of the class. Please notify Lateral Fitness if you are running late. We will release your reservation if you are not present at the start of class.

Cancellations

Do to our limit class sizes (15-20) Yoga (15-20) Pilates (8-10) Bootcamps, it is imperative for you to cancel your scheduled appointment within 12 hours of class time. If you fail to cancel within the allotted time you will be charged for the single price of the class or if you have purchased a package you will forfeit a session.
