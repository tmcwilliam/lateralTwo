6 Week Challenge

The 6 Week Challenge with Adrienne is a diet bootcamp. It includes one group personal training session a week plus personal weekly nutritional guidance. You WILL lose weight, inches and body fat. All participants who finish the challenge receive a gift bag. Participant who loses the most body fat plus inches wins 3 personal training sessions or 30% off an 8 class package plus gift bag. Please contact Lateral for more info and to sign up.
