Erik Marthaler

Erik Marthaler, co-founder of Lateral Fitness, has over 12 years of experience in the fitness world. He has a Bachelors in Science degree with a focus of Kinesiology: Exercise Science and Fitness from Illinois State University. Erik is a Team USA Triathlete and Duathlete, has a USAT (USA Triathlon) Level 1, USAC Level 3 Certified Coach, USATF (USA Track and Field), ACE (American Council in Exercise), HKC Kettlebell, and a TRX certification, Erik has the knowledge and talents to work with everyone from post-surgery rehabilitation, pre and post natal, cardiac rehab patients to training clients for fitness shows, Ironman races and a specialist in clients to want weight lost.
